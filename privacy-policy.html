Privacy Policy

Welcome to our privacy policy page. Below, we explain how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our partner broker services. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this privacy policy carefully to understand how we handle your personal data.

I. Collection and Use of Information

1.1 Personal Data

We collect various types of information for different purposes in order to provide and improve our partner broker services.

1.1.1 Information You Provide

When you use our partner broker services or interact with us, we may ask you to provide certain personal data that can be used to identify or contact you ("personal data"). Personal data may include, but is not limited to:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Postal address
  • Cookies and usage data

1.1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may also collect information on how our partner broker services are accessed and used ("usage data"). Usage data may include information such as your IP address, browser type, browser version, pages of our services that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, and other diagnostic data.

1.1.3 Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information and improve your experience with our partner broker services. Cookies are small files that contain a unique identifier and are transferred from a web server to your computer’s hard drive. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some parts of our services.

II. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose your personal data for various reasons, including but not limited to:

  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To protect and defend our rights or property.
  • To prevent or investigate potential violations of our terms of use.

III. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to improve our partner broker services or to provide specific features. These third parties may have access to your personal data in order to perform these tasks on our behalf, but are obligated not to disclose or use that information for any other purpose.

IV. Google Ads

Our partner broker services may use Google Ads, an advertising service from Google LLC ("Google"). Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to our website and other websites across the internet. You can opt-out of Google’s use of cookies by accessing the Google Ads settings.

V. Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, update, correct, or delete your personal data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below. We will process your request as soon as possible.

VI. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed. By continuing to use our services after changes are posted, you agree to those changes.
